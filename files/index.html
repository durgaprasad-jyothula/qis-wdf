The Department of Computer Science & engineering was started in the year 1998. It has become the most sought after department among engineering aspirants. The college conducts the examinations and the degree is awarded by JNTU Kakinada. University incorporates latest developments in Basic Computer Science, Programming, Application development, Communication, Datamining and Datawarehousing and allied fields in a dynamic fashion so that the student is exposed to the latest technological advancements during the program. The Department is being accredited By NBA Committee of AICTE In the year 2009. This was a great achievement in the history of the department. The department is also offering the Two PG programmes, M.Tech in Computer Science and M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering.
